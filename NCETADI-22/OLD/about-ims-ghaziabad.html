About IMS Ghaziabad

IMS Ghaziabad, one of the trusted Business Schools in India with 32 years of academic legacy offers PGDM programme which is approved by AICTE and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and equivalent to MBA by Association of Indian Universities (AIU). The Institute is also accredited globally by ASIC, U.K, and is a proud member of AACSB Business Education Alliance, USA.

The Institute is consistently conferred with numerous prestigious awards such as “Best Management Institute in North India for Industry Interface” by CEGR in December 2021, “Excellence in Education” award during Higher Education Summit held in December 2021; "Best Institute in India with Strong Industry Connect" conferred by Federation For World Academics (FWA), October 2021; "Institutional Award for Excellence in Placements & Skill Development” during LEAD Global Conclave in September 2021; “Contribution to Innovation in Management Education” award by Commonwealth Thought Leaders in June 2021; “Most Trusted Management Institute of the Year” by Top Gallant Media, March 2021 and many more. It has earned exceptional rankings of “7th Private Standalone B-School” in All India, “10th Top Private B-School” in Delhi NCR, “12th Top Private B-School” in North Zone in survey of India’s Best B Schools 2022 conducted by ‘OUTLOOK’ “8th Top Private B School” in the CSR-GHRDC B-Schools Survey 2021; “7th in Private B Schools in Delhi NCR”, “8th in Overall Delhi NCR”, “11th in Private B Schools North Zone” in the survey of India’s Best B Schools conducted by ‘THE WEEK’; “14th Top B-School in All India”, “6th Top Private Institutes in All India” and “6th in North India” by Times B-School Survey 2020.

IMS Ghaziabad is the first Higher Educational Institute in Delhi and NCR to be awarded with the elite ISO 21001:2018 Certification, for its conformity to the requirements of the Management Systems for Educational Organisations (EOMS) standard, for its PGDM Two Year Full Time Program in October 2021.

To aid students in achieving their education and career goals, the Institute provides meritorious scholarships in various categories amounting 2.70 Crores so as to remove financial barriers and fuel intellectual capital. IMS Ghaziabad is located in the heart of city and is within 5 Km radius from bus stand, railway station and metro station of Ghaziabad.

IMS Ghaziabad feels honoured to be associated with 6000+ Alumni base that are well established occupying top positions in the industry. Our students have made us proud by doing exceptionally well in their entrepreneurial ventures too. With a strong industry-academia interface, we cherish our association with a renowned galaxy of recruiters and are proudly able to accomplish stellar placements in A Listed organizations. With its tripartite focus on fostering Academic Excellence, Corporate Readiness and Global Interface, IMS Ghaziabad has been consistently reinventing management education and has attained a unique distinction and an elite place amongst the premier professional educational institutions in India.
