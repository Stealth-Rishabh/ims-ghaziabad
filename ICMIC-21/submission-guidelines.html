How to Submit

Please submit your extended abstract and full paper by Easy Chair as per the instructions given below. For any other query you can write to: ICMIC21@imsgzb.ac.in

Submission Guidelines:

Contributors are requested to submit the paper as per the guidelines given below:

The first page should contain
  • Manuscripts will be accepted in electronic format through EasyChair web management
  • https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icmic21
  • A separate title page should include an informative title, name(s), affiliations, address(es) of Institute/Organization, corresponding address, email id(s), contact no(s) of all authors
  • Please do not indicate author(s) name, affiliation, or any other such information in the manuscripts elsewhere
  • Abstract should be of 100-300 words followed by a list of 5-6 keywords which are to be written in alphabetical order
  • Manuscript should be of 1500-3000 words approximately, excluding tables, graphs & charts
  • All text should be in 1.5 space with 1-inch margins on all the sides on A4 size paper using the Font
  • Times New Roman of size 12
  • All charts, graphs, pictures should be drawn and labeled legibly in MS Word
  • References should be arranged in alphabetical order and as per the APA (American Psychological Association) Guidelines. It is imperative to ensure that all works cited in the text are included in the References section.
  • All manuscript would be checked for plagiarism.
  • Footnotes should be avoided, but any short, succinct notes making a specific point may be placed in number order following the alphabetical list of references.
  • References should be made only to works that are published, accepted for publication (not merely "submitted"), or available through libraries or institutions. Any other source should be qualified by a note regarding availability.
