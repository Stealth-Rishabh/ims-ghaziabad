About The Conference

Institute of Management Studies, Ghaziabad is organizing a two-day AICTE Sponsored National Conference on “Emerging Technologies in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science and Internet of Things (NCETADI-22)” during May 20-21, 2022. The Conference aims to bring together leading corporate practitioners, data scientists, researchers and academic scholars to exchange their experiences, learning and research related to all aspects of Technological Innovation in different functional areas of Intelligent computing, Network and Social computing, Data and Cloud computing, Computer Algorithms and Applications, System and Software Engineering aspects of contemporary computing.
