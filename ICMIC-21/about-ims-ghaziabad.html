About IMS Ghaziabad

IMS Ghaziabad is a 31 years old flagship institute of IMS Society Ghaziabad and one of the oldest B School in India which is accredited by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) with 'A' Grade and Globally accreditated by ASIC, U.K and is a proud member of AACSB Business Education Alliance, USA. The PGDM Programme of IMS Ghaziabad is being accreditated by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and equivalend to MBA by Association of Indian University (AIU). The institute has signed various MoUs with MCX (India Largest commodity derivatives exchange), BRICS CCI School of Analysis, CSC Academy, IBM, Northern Illinois University, Fort Hays State University, Fredericton University of Canada and Jamia Hamdard University, Delhi to boost research, students & faculty development. IMS Ghaziabad has been ranked amongst Top 10 best B-Schools in North India as per latest MBA and B School Rankings.
