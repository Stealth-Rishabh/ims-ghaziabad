Call for Papers

The National Conference, sponsored by AICTE on “Emerging Technologies in Artificial Intelligence,Data Science and Internet of Things (NCETADI-22)”,would provide a forum for practitioners, policymakers, academicians, researchers, and students to share their practical experiences, knowledge and insight into next-generation technological innovation used in Artificial Intelligence, crypto-currencies, advanced automation, deep learning.

Prospective authors are invited to submit original research papers and case studies for presentation at the conference on the below-mentioned sub-themes or any topic related to sub-themes.

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. Please submit your abstract and full paper in electronic format through the Easy Chair web management link https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ncetadi22. For any other query, you can write to: conference2022@imsgzb.ac.in
